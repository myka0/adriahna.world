About me!

Education

B.S. in Microbiology/Cell and Molecular Biology from Oklahoma State University

Research Interests

My undergraduate research focused on bacterial molecular genetics, exploring the regulation of pyocin production by Pseudomonas aeruginosa. In my future studies, I hope to explore bacteria-host interactions in the gut microbiome.

Art

I mostly create digital art, but dabble in sketching and acrylics. Currently, I am exploring different avenues to combine my love of art with my love of science. That is why I am working on an animated Youtube series! Combining engaging story telling and appealing art, I hope to bridge the world of science (particuarly microbiology) with the general public in a meaningful way. Stay tuned for more!
